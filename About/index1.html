What is our mission?

Helping Hand's aim is to promote positive mental health and wellness in all individuals and communities, and we assist those who are experiencing mental health challenges on their road to recovery.

We work to increase community awareness of mental health and the mind-body connection between mental and physical health. We also provide knowledge and chances for people to recognize early warning signs of mental illness.


What is mental health?

Being mentally healthy implies being able to deal with life's daily problems and understanding your own overall well-being. Being mentally well entails far more than simply being free of mental health issues.

Our emotional, psychological, and social well-being all contribute to our mental health. Mental health is essential at all stages of life, from childhood through adolescent to adulthood.
